Chairman’s Welcome Message

Mr. Zubair Ghangra, Chairman

Assalam-o-Alaikum and a warm welcome!

On behalf of the Hyderabad SITE Association of Trade & Industry, I extend my heartfelt gratitude to all our members for placing their confidence in me and electing me as your Chairman for the 2025–2026 tenure.

We stand today at a critical juncture: Hyderabad’s SITE industrial area grapples with longstanding infrastructure challenges—ranging from deteriorated roads and ineffective drainage to erratic supply of electricity, gas, and water, as well as security concerns—that are impeding the productivity and growth of our member industries.

During my tenure, my core focus will be:

  • Infrastructure rehabilitation: Coordination with Municipal Corporation, WASA, SSGC, SEPCO, and relevant agencies.
  • Ensuring uninterrupted utilities: Working with regulators to resolve supply challenges.
  • Strengthening security: Collaboration with law enforcement and industrial security.
  • Institutional coordination: Using SITE Works Committee and provincial links for development.
  • Member engagement: Encouraging open suggestions and feedback from industry.

We are committed to improving operational efficiencies and enhancing the overall ecosystem of SITE Hyderabad.

I invite all member businesses to engage actively. Your ideas are invaluable—whether in policy advocacy, development, or industry promotion.

Thank you for your trust and support. I look forward to working with you toward building a stronger, more resilient industrial community in SITE Hyderabad.

Warm Regards,

Zubair Gangra

Chairman, Hyderabad SITE Association of Trade & Industry

2025–2026

Senior Vice Chairman’s Message

Mr. Amir Shahab
Senior Vice Chairman, Hyderabad SITE Association of Trade & Industry
Tenure: 2025–2026

Assalam-o-Alaikum,

It is a privilege to serve as the Senior Vice Chairman of the Hyderabad SITE Association of Trade & Industry for the 2025–2026 term. I extend my sincere thanks to the business community for this opportunity to serve.

Hyderabad’s industrial potential is immense—but it demands urgent attention to core issues like infrastructure, drainage, and utility services. Under the leadership of our Chairman Mr. Zubair Ghangra, we are committed to practical solutions and strong coordination with all relevant authorities.

My goal is to assist in ensuring timely resolution of members’ problems and to help create a more business-friendly environment in SITE Hyderabad. Together, with your active participation, we will work toward growth, sustainability, and better services for all.

Your trust is our strength.
