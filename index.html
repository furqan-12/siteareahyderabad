MANAGEMENT

The policies and programs of the Hyderabad SITE Association of Trade and Industry are formulated by an 11-member Managing Committee, elected by the General Body for a tenure of two years. The Managing Committee elects the Chairman, Senior Vice Chairman, Vice Chairman, General Secretary, and Finance Secretary. The Chairman oversees all operational and administrative matters of the Association, assisted by the Senior Vice Chairman and Vice Chairman. To ensure efficient office functioning, the Managing Committee also appoints an Office Secretary, responsible for official correspondence, record-keeping, and the coordination of meetings. At the conclusion of each tenure, fresh elections are held to constitute a new Managing Committee, thereby continuing the democratic and representative governance of the Association.
