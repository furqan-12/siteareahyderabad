Latifabad, Hyderabad - SITE Area

The SITE (Sindh Industrial Trading Estate) Area is strategically located in Latifabad, Hyderabad, providing easy access to major transportation routes and industrial facilities. Our association office is situated in the heart of this industrial zone, serving as a central hub for trade and industry activities.

Electricity

Most industries in SITE Area Hyderabad get their electricity from HESCO (Hyderabad Electric Supply Company). Some large units may also use backup generators or solar panels for uninterrupted power supply.

Water

Water supply for industries is mainly provided by SITE (Sindh Industrial and Trade Estate) and supplemented by bore wells or private tankers, depending on the company's requirements and location within SITE Area.
